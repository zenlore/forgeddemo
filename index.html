About Flexible Dieting

Flexible dieting allows you to cater to your demanding schedule while you still reach your fitness goals. With access to personal programming and freedom to eat your favorite foods, nutrition is no longer burdensome. Not familiar with flexible dieting? Keep scrolling to learn more!

How It Works

After receiving your filled out questionnaire I will analyze your information and determine your personalized macros. Every week you will check in via email to ensure that your new diet is going as planned. In the event that your progress stalls, I will reassess your macros and adjust them accordingly. I urge clients to be detailed in their check in's so I can not only maximize their diet plan, but our coach/client relationship as well.
